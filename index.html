Korea's Finest Hotel

Hotel Antique is a heritage hotel located in the heart of Seoul, the thriving capital city, in a prime location that is minutes walking distance from major cultural landmarks. Our location is in a commercial area with high-end shops and a variety of exquisite Korean and international dining options. Our 5-star deluxe luxury property is conveniently situated 18 KM from Incheon International Airport, about 2 KM from the famous shopping district of Myeongdong.
